## Understanding Kratom Quality Indicators

### Alkaloid Content and Potency

The primary alkaloids in kratom, mitragynine and 7-hydroxymitragynine, determine product effectiveness. Quality vendors test alkaloid percentages and share this information with customers.

Premium kratom typically contains:

- 1.2-1.8% mitragynine content
- 0.01-0.04% 7-hydroxymitragynine
- Consistent alkaloid profiles within strains

Lower alkaloid percentages may indicate old kratom, poor processing, or inferior leaf quality.

### Color, Texture, and Aroma

Visual and sensory inspection reveals quality differences:

**Color**: Fresh kratom powder displays vibrant, consistent coloring matching its vein type. Dull, brownish powder suggests age or improper storage.

**Texture**: Premium kratom features fine, fluffy powder without clumps or excessive stems. Coarse, gritty texture indicates inadequate processing.

**Aroma**: Quality kratom produces a distinct, earthy scent. Musty or moldy odors signal contamination or moisture damage.
